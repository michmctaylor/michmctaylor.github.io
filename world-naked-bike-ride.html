The World Naked Bike Ride

The World Naked Bike Ride is an annual event aimed to build awareness for our current dependence on fossil fuels and to attract more attention by encouraging full or partial nudity. Event participators often wear costumes or paint their bodies with images or phrases while riding. The event is a peaceful protest and becomes more and more popular each year. WNBR is a non-profit world wide collective of environmentally conscious individuals devoted to changing the world’s energy production and consumption policies.

San Francisco and Chicago are where the two largest naked bike rides take place in the country each year. A unique hand drawn and illustrated poster was created for each city to exhibit the different characteristics of the cities and their respective events. The event usually takes place during the warmer summer months, so the combination of bright complementary colors was chosen, one for each city. The posters are printed on a transparent smooth vellum paper, which relates to the riders’ naked bodies and their bare all attitudes.
